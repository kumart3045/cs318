Chicken 65

Ingredients

  • 500 grams Chicken
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Juice
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tablespoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon Garam Masala Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cumin Powder
  • 2 tablespoon Corn Flour
  • 1 tablespoon Rice Flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Sunflower Oil for deep frying
  • 2 green chillis
  • 10 curry leaves

Instructions

  1. Clean and cut chicken into 1-inch cubes
  2. In a bowl, add the chicken, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chilli powder, garam masala, coriander powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, cornflour, rice flour, egg, green chilli, curry leaves and salt. mix this well and marinate the chicken in thsi mixture for 60 minutes
  3. Add oil in a deep frying pan, heat the oil and deep fry the marinated chicken in batched for 5-6 minutes
  4. Serve hot
